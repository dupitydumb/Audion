# Getting Started with Audion

## Listen to Local Music

1. Open Audion.
2. Click **"Add Music Folder"** in the sidebar (or on the home screen on mobile).
3. Select the folder where your music is stored.
4. Audion will scan your files and organize them by songs, albums, and artists — done!

> Supports MP3, FLAC, AAC, OGG, WAV, and more.

---

## Stream Music with Tidal

Want to stream from Tidal? Install the **Tidal Search** plugin:

1. Go to **Settings > Plugins > Marketplace**.
2. Find **Tidal Search** and click **Install**.
3. Enable the plugin.
4. Click the **Tidal** button in the player bar to open the search panel.
5. Search for any song or artist and click a result to start streaming.

### Save a Track

Click the **♥ (love) button** on any Tidal track to save it to your Audion library.

---

That's it — enjoy your music! 🎧
