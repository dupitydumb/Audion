# Release Notes - v1.2.5

We are excited to announce the release of Audion v1.2.5! This update introduces several highly requested features, including account synchronization, enhanced metadata integration, and improved library management tools.

## 🚀 Key Features

### ☁️ Account Synchronization
*   **Multi-Device Sync:** Stay connected across all your devices. Your library, playlists, and settings now automatically synchronize via our new account service.

### 🔍 Metadata & Discovery
*   **MusicBrainz & ListenBrainz Integration:** Enjoy improved track matching and scrobbling with native support for MusicBrainz and ListenBrainz metadata providers.
*   **Custom Artwork:** Take full control of your library's visual identity by uploading and assigning custom artwork to any track or album.

### 📂 Library Management
*   **Enhanced Pinning:** Keep your favorite content within reach by pinning playlists, albums, and artists directly to your navigation.
*   **Advanced Sorting:** Quickly find your newest additions with the new "Sort by Date Added" functionality.

### 🎵 Playback & Lyrics
*   **Offline Lyrics Support:** You can now download and store lyrics locally, ensuring they are available for viewing even without an active internet connection.

## 🔧 Bug Fixes
*   **PIP Mode (Mini Player):** Resolved an issue where the Picture-in-Picture window would not correctly resize or maintain state.
